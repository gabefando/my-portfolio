About me

Hello and thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio website. I have been coding for a couple of weeks now and have been learning HTML, CSS, and most recently JavaScript. I was born in 2000 in Atlanta GA. I decided to learn programming because it is something that has always interested me and I have tried to self learn it over the years. I decided to join a bootcamp and learn it for real and it is going well.
